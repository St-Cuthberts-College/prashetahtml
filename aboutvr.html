About virtual reality

What is virtual reality (VR)?

Virtual reality is a simulated envruonment users can be immersed in to have experiences digitally.

Is Augumented Reality similar to VR?

Virtual Reality is an environment that is computer generated and you can be fully immersed in it by wearing headsets. Augmented reality is where you can use your real environment and add digital elements to it through a screen.
